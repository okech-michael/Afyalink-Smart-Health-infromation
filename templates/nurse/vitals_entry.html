Priority guide

High

O2 < 90%, temp > 39.5°C, BP > 180/110 or < 90/60, pulse > 140 or < 40, severe pain (8–10), unconscious, chest pain, active bleeding, critical severity reported.

Medium

Moderate symptoms, stable vitals but patient distressed, pain score 4–7, moderate severity reported.

Low

All vitals within normal range, mild symptoms, routine follow-up, vaccination, administrative visit.
