How referrals work

  1. Submit this form. The patient record is instantly visible at the receiving facility.
  2. The receiving reception desk sees the referral in their incoming queue.
  3. The receiving doctor opens the consultation with the patient's full history already loaded.
  4. No paper, no repeat registration, no lost records.

Facility levels guide

L2 Dispensary — basic outpatient
L3 Health centre — maternity, minor surgery
L4 Sub-county hospital — inpatient, lab, pharmacy
L5 County hospital — specialists, ICU
L6 Referral hospital — complex surgery, oncology
